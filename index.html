About Me

I am Abdulazeez Raheem, from Lagos, Nigeria, currently pursuing a Master’s in Sustainable International Agriculture at the University of Göttingen in Germany. Previously, I earned a Master’s in Animal Science from the University of Agriculture Abeokuta, Nigeria, where my focus was on sustainable livestock production. With a keen interest in technology, I am enhancing my skills in front-end development through IT classes, having already learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My aim is to integrate technology with sustainable agriculture to develop innovative solutions that boost productivity and sustainability. Passionate about community development, I volunteer with NGOs in Lagos, promoting agricultural education among young farmers. My vision is to leverage technology to create resilient global food systems.

Motivation

It has always been my goal to combine my expertise in sustainable agriculture with modern technology, creating innovative solutions that enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability. I am particularly interested in developing user-friendly applications and platforms that can empower farmers with real-time data, insights, and tools to optimize their agricultural practices.
